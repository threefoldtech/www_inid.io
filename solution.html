Initial Steps towards Global Citizenship

There are more than 3 billion people in the world who have no digital presence today. They have no or limited access to the internet, no access to reliable banking services and are not ready to contribute in the global digital ecosystem. A digital identification will allow users to obtain a verifiable credential that can be used for other services without sharing more than what is required, especially not the underlying data that comprises the credential.
