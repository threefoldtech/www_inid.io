About Us

IINID is funded by Incubaid, an incubator focused primarily on helping to change our planet for the better through technology.

We develop human-centered technology, embedding responsibility in the design, core values and code. 

The group resources add up to 70 people worldwide.
We have presence in Istanbul, Ankara, Dubai, Cairo and Lochristi.

We share a common framework called FreeFlow. Find out more at  http://www.freeflownation.org
