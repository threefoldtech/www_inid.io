INID is an impact-focused startup born out of Incubaid, an incubator focused primarily on helping to change our planet for the better through technology. 

We use develop human-centered technology, embedding responsibility in the design, core values and code, based on ThreeFold.Tech's open source self-driving technology.

We share a common framework called FreeFlow.
